## Anomaly Report: ANOM-047

**Classification:** Class-B  
**Status:** Under Investigation  
**Discovery Date:** March 15, 1995  
**Location:** Arkham, Massachusetts  
**Lead Investigator:** Marcus Chen

## Description

ANOM-047 refers to a wooden door located in the basement of an abandoned textile mill in Arkham. The door exhibits several anomalous properties:

1. The door produces audible whispers in an unknown language when approached
2. The door's location within the building changes unpredictably
3. Attempts to open the door have resulted in temporary auditory hallucinations
4. The door appears to be significantly older than the building itself

## Investigation History

- **March 15, 1995:** Initial discovery by local urban explorers
- **March 18, 1995:** Site secured by Eidolon Capital field team
- **April 2, 1995:** First analysis attempt - inconclusive results
- **May 10, 1995:** Door relocated itself 50 meters from original position
- **June 1, 1995:** Dr. Harrow recommended Class-B classification

## Current Status

The anomaly remains contained within the secured building. Regular monitoring is in place. Further investigation is pending approval from the Research Committee.

## Safety Protocols

- Minimum distance of 3 meters must be maintained
- Audio dampening equipment required for all interactions
- Maximum exposure time: 15 minutes per person per day
- No lone personnel allowed within containment area

## Notes

The language of the whispers has been tentatively identified as resembling Ancient Sumerian, but with significant deviations. Linguistic analysis is ongoing.
