## Overview

Miskatonic University is one of the oldest and most prestigious institutions of higher learning in New England. Known for its extensive library and occult collection, the university has been a valuable research partner to Eidolon Capital since our founding.

## Areas of Collaboration

- Ancient language translation
- Historical research and archival access
- Theoretical physics consultation
- Archaeological expedition support
- Occult text analysis

## Partnership Agreement

Current partnership agreement signed in 1992, renewable annually. Eidolon Capital provides funding for specific research projects in exchange for priority access to findings and resources.

## Security Clearance

Select faculty members hold Level 2 clearance for consultation on non-critical anomalies.

## Notes

The university library contains several texts that have proven invaluable in anomaly research. Regular consultation meetings occur quarterly. The university administration is aware of our operations at a high level but not of specific anomaly details.
